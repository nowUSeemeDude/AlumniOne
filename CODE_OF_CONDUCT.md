# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone participating in this project.

All contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to follow this code of conduct to ensure respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

* Be respectful and considerate in communication
* Provide constructive feedback
* Focus on improving the project
* Respect differing opinions and experiences

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

* Harassment or discrimination
* Personal attacks or insulting language
* Public or private harassment
* Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional environment

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate action when violations occur.

Violations may result in:

* Warning
* Temporary restriction
* Permanent removal from the project community
